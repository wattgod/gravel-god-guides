The Plan

Element Details
Limiter Identified Power-to-weight ratio - Need 4.5% improvement (4.4 → 4.6 w/kg) to achieve 4% speed increase on 8.2% grade
Practices
  • Threshold intervals (2x/week) - Build sustained power at FTP
  • Long tempo climbs (1x/week) - Specificity for 25-min climbing efforts
  • Body composition optimization - Lose 4 lbs while maintaining/gaining power
Weekly Actions
  • Tuesday: 2x20min @ FTP with 10min recovery
  • Thursday: 3x12min @ 105% FTP with 6min recovery
  • Saturday: 90min ride with 40min continuous tempo climb (85-90% FTP)
  • Daily: 300 calorie deficit + 1g protein per lb bodyweight
Progression Timeline
  • Weeks 1-8: Build base threshold power + establish calorie deficit
  • Weeks 9-16: Increase interval intensity, maintain deficit
  • Weeks 17-20: Taper volume, hold weight, peak for attempt
  • Weeks 21-24: Final taper and attempt window
